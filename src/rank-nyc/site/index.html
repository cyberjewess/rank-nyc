HOW DOES THE BALLOT WORK?

You can rank UP TO 5 people in order of preference! And you do NOT have to fill out your entire ballot.

HOW ARE VOTES COUNTED?

Count 1st choice votes. If someone passes 50%, they win!

Otherwise: Drop the last place candidate and move those ballots to each voter's next choice, if they have one. Repeat until a candidate passes 50%.
